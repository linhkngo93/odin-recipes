Medium Rare Steak

Summary:

This is an easy way to cook steak at home

  • 8 oz steak
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. season steak with salt and pepper
  2. heat pan with butters until hot
  3. add steak and garlic into pan
  4. cook each sides for 3 minutes for medium rare
  5. let the steak rest and serve after 2 minutes
