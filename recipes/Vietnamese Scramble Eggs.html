Vietnamese Scramble Eggs

Summary

This is a traditional Vietnamese comfort food for me

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tps fish sauce
  • 1/2 tps sugar
  • 1 tps garlic
  1. crack eggs
  2. add all condiments into egg bowl
  3. wisk eggs until all mix thoroughly
  4. cook eggs on medium heat with cooking oil
  5. serve egg
